# Arcadia Crypto User Guides

## 1. How to Create an Account

Creating an account on Arcadia Crypto is quick and secure. Follow these steps to get started:

1. Visit the Arcadia Crypto website (www.arcadiacrypto.com) and click on the "Sign Up" button in the top right corner.

2. Enter your email address and create a strong password. Your password should be at least 12 characters long and include a mix of uppercase and lowercase letters, numbers, and special characters.

3. Agree to the Terms of Service and Privacy Policy.

4. Click "Create Account".

5. Check your email for a verification link. Click the link to verify your email address.

6. Once verified, log in to your new account.

7. Set up two-factor authentication (2FA) for enhanced security:
   - Go to "Account Settings" > "Security"
   - Choose between Google Authenticator, SMS, or hardware key options
   - Follow the prompts to set up your chosen 2FA method

8. Complete the Know Your Customer (KYC) process:
   - Go to "Account Settings" > "Verification"
   - Provide your full name, date of birth, and address
   - Upload a clear photo of your government-issued ID (passport, driver's license, or national ID card)
   - Take a selfie following the on-screen instructions
   - Submit your information for review (typically processed within 1-2 business days)

9. Once your account is verified, you're ready to start trading!

## 2. Depositing Funds

Arcadia Crypto supports various methods for depositing funds:

### Fiat Currency Deposits:

1. Go to "Wallet" > "Deposit" > "Fiat"
2. Select your preferred fiat currency (USD, EUR, GBP, etc.)
3. Choose a deposit method:
   - Bank Transfer (ACH/SEPA)
   - Wire Transfer
   - Credit/Debit Card
   - PayPal (where available)
4. Follow the instructions for your chosen method
5. For bank transfers, use the provided bank details and include your unique reference number
6. Wait for the funds to be credited to your account (processing times vary by method)

### Cryptocurrency Deposits:

1. Go to "Wallet" > "Deposit" > "Crypto"
2. Select the cryptocurrency you wish to deposit
3. Generate a new deposit address or use an existing one
4. Send your cryptocurrency to the provided address
5. Wait for the required number of blockchain confirmations
6. Once confirmed, the funds will be credited to your trading account

Note: Always double-check the deposit address before sending any cryptocurrency.

## 3. Placing Trade Orders

Arcadia Crypto offers various order types to suit different trading strategies:

### Market Order:

1. Go to the trading page for your desired trading pair (e.g., BTC/USD)
2. In the order form, select "Market" order
3. Enter the amount you want to buy or sell
4. Review the estimated price and total
5. Click "Buy" or "Sell" to execute the order immediately at the best available market price

### Limit Order:

1. Select "Limit" order in the order form
2. Enter the amount you want to buy or sell
3. Set your desired limit price
4. Choose the time-in-force option (Good-til-Cancelled, Fill-or-Kill, etc.)
5. Click "Buy" or "Sell" to place the order
6. The order will execute when the market reaches your specified price

### Stop-Limit Order:

1. Select "Stop-Limit" in the order form
2. Enter the amount you want to buy or sell
3. Set the stop price (triggers the order) and limit price (maximum/minimum price)
4. Choose the time-in-force option
5. Click "Buy" or "Sell" to place the order
6. The order will be triggered at the stop price and executed at the limit price or better

### Advanced Order Types:

For OCO (One-Cancels-the-Other) orders, trailing stop orders, and other advanced types, refer to the detailed trading guide in our Help Center.

## 4. Using Advanced Trading Tools

Arcadia Crypto provides a suite of advanced tools to enhance your trading experience:

### TradingView Charts:

1. On the trading page, locate the chart section
2. Use the toolbar above the chart to access drawing tools and indicators
3. Click the "Indicators" button to add technical indicators (e.g., MACD, RSI)
4. Use the time frame selector to change the chart's time frame
5. Right-click on the chart to access more options, including saving layouts

### Order Book Analysis:

1. Find the order book on the trading page
2. Use the depth chart to visualize buy and sell pressure
3. Adjust the aggregation level to change the grouping of orders
4. Hover over price levels to see the cumulative volume

### Portfolio Tracker:

1. Go to "Portfolio" in the main navigation
2. View your current holdings, open orders, and trade history
3. Use the performance analytics to track your ROI and trading patterns
4. Set up price alerts for specific assets or portfolio value thresholds

## 5. Withdrawing Funds

### Fiat Currency Withdrawals:

1. Go to "Wallet" > "Withdraw" > "Fiat"
2. Select the fiat currency you wish to withdraw
3. Choose your withdrawal method (bank transfer, wire, etc.)
4. Enter the withdrawal amount and recipient bank details
5. Review the transaction details and fees
6. Confirm the withdrawal using 2FA
7. Wait for the withdrawal to be processed (1-3 business days typically)

### Cryptocurrency Withdrawals:

1. Go to "Wallet" > "Withdraw" > "Crypto"
2. Select the cryptocurrency you wish to withdraw
3. Enter the recipient's wallet address (double-check for accuracy!)
4. Specify the amount to withdraw
5. Review the network fee and final amount
6. Confirm the withdrawal using 2FA
7. Wait for the transaction to be processed and confirmed on the blockchain

## 6. Security Best Practices

To keep your Arcadia Crypto account secure:

1. Enable 2FA and use a hardware security key if possible
2. Use a unique, strong password for your Arcadia Crypto account
3. Never share your login credentials or 2FA codes with anyone
4. Be wary of phishing attempts - always check the URL before logging in
5. Use the whitelisting feature for withdrawal addresses
6. Regularly review your account activity and enable email notifications
7. Keep your computer and mobile devices secure and up-to-date
